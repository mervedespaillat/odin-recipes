Kale Salad

Description

Healthy and fast recipe for a light dinner.

Ingredients

  • Chopped kale
  • 3 Mandarins
  • Feta Cheese
  • Sliced almond
  • 3 Tbs olive oil
  • 3 Tbs lemon juice
  • 1 Tbs balsamic vinegar
  • 1 Tbs honey
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Put kale, sliced and cut mandarins, shredded feta cheese in a bowl
  2. Mixed olive oil, lemon juice, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt and pepper in a different cup
  3. Pour the oil mix on to the salad
  4. Sprinkle the sliced almond
