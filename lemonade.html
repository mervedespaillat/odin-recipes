Lemonade Recipe

Description

What is better then freshly made a lemonade in a hot summer day. This is the very basic but still very delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of cold water
  • 2 cups of lemon juice
  • 2 cup of sugar
  • Sliced strawberries
  • Ice
  • Fresh mint

Steps

  1. Mix water, lemon juice and sugar in large pitcher until sugar is dissolved. Refrigerate about 3 hours or until chilled if desired.
  2. Serve lemonade over ice. Garnish each serving with a strawberry slice and mint.
