Artichoke Recipe

Description

Total time is 20 mins. It's an easy and fast refreshing summer dish.
Artichokes are low in fat while rich in fiber, vitamins, minerals and antioxidants which these are make it a good option for diet.
Let's see what we need to do this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of artichokes bottom
  • 1 can of garniture
  • 3 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Juice of a lemon
  • A branch of dill

Steps

  1. Place the artichokes on a pan
  2. Put the garniture on top of artichokes
  3. Put olive oil on top of everything with lemon juice
  4. Let is sit for 15 mins and serve with dill
